The Origin Story

"It started with a slow dental clinic..."

In 2023, I (Akash) walked into a local dental practice. The doctor was brilliant, the clinic was pristine, but the waiting room was empty. I asked why. He sighed and pointed to his iPad. "We paid an agency $5,000 for this website, but nobody calls."

I opened the site on my phone. It took 14 seconds to load. The "Book Now" button was broken on mobile. It wasn't a website; it was a digital wall keeping patients out.

That night, I tore his site down. I didn't use WordPress. I didn't use a template. I hand-coded a raw HTML engine tailored for speed.

The result? His phone rang 12 times the next day. In one month, his practice was full. That was the moment Zyrova was born. Not as an agency, but as a rebellion against lazy, bloated, template-based web design.

- Akash Jadon, Founder
